about us

Cuppa Joe coffee shop offers a unique coffee house environment unlike any other in Small Town, U.S.A! We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of java (although you are more than welcome to do just that), we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee. If you need to work, we have a seating area created specifically for you. If you need to rest, we have a soft-seating area in the back that is perfect for your weary mind and body. If you are looking for entertainment, we have rotating roster of events and music sure to please a variety of tastes.

In addition, we offer a delicious variety of locally grown coffee made by our professionally trained baristas. Don't know what to order? Let them know what your taste buds crave and they will be happy to offer up a suggestion. We have everything from classic coffee to our daily house made specialty beverages. Finally, don't forget to complete your coffee experience with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker. Come on down. We can't wait to see you at Cuppa Joe!
